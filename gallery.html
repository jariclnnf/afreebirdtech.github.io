A Free Bird™ is committed to helping every child develop the strength and willpower to fight their disease. Cancer is a physical disease that can sap the energy of some of the strongest children. We are committed to helping them keep their spirits strong and never lose hope for their future. All art forms are empowering and we believe we can help the doctors and nurses to heal each child through the arts. Join us in our mission and lets “Empower each bird to fly free with the arts”.
